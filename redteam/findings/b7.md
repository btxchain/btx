### Honest-clipping risk (does drift=1800 or 900 ever clip honest blocks?)
The honest miner **self-clamps** (`UpdateTime` sets nTime=min(now, prev_mtp+drift)), so an honest block is **NEVER rejected** for
any drift — it is only down-stamped. block.time−prev_mtp at steady 90s spacing = **max 540s** (this run). Mainnet live max = 1143s (LEARNINGS).
```

drift= 900: 1143 > 900   => clips the TAIL (~0.7% of blocks down-stamped); benign MTP distortion, NO rejection/liveness loss.
```
⇒ **drift=1800 is the safe tightening** (zero honest clipping, caps per-block lever to ×1.41 and peak to ×2.7). 900 over-tightens.
